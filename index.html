Storage Sync Status

🟢

Server Sync is Active!

Your data is fully secure and backed up physically.

✨ Permanent Storage: Every time you add, edit, or delete transactions, or update your lock PIN, changes are automatically saved to data.json on your computer's hard drive.

🔒 Complete Safeguard: Browser cleanups, incognito mode, or browser crashes will never wipe your credit card records or security PIN anymore.

⚠️

Browser Storage Only (Temporary)

Your data is at risk of being reset by your web browser.

WARNING: Incognito tabs, disk cleanup software, browser history clearings, or browser updates will completely wipe your ledger data and lock PIN.

How to activate permanent persistent sync on your PC:

  1. Open your project folder: d:\Projects\CreditCardTracker
  2. Find and double-click the file named run-ledger.bat.
  3. This starts a local background sync server and launches the secure ledger on http://localhost:3000.

Note: If this app is deployed on a static hosting service, you can also perform manual secure backups using the 📥/📤 (CSV Export/Import) buttons in the header dashboard at any time.

If you open the online deployed website on your phone, enter your PC's IP address (shown in the black batch launcher window) to sync phone data directly to your computer!
